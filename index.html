About

Digital transformation is not simply about making the journey to the cloud. The digital business era has forced organizations to drive change through strategically aligned IT and business goals. As such, organizations need to address their cloud computing needs by effectively integrating and coordinating their digital transformation efforts to develop an efficient cloud strategy for their entire enterprise.

Aureus Technologies helps clients effectively automate their IT infrastructure through the cloud by combining best practices in configuration management, continuous integration, and cloud cost optimization with a portfolio of cloud technology partners, custom solutions and IT governance policies.

As your AWS cloud partner, Aureus Technologies ensures that you leverage the Amazon Web Services cloud infrastructure by helping you build, deploy, and manage your cloud architectures on AWS.

  • High availability and performance
  • Increased agility and transparency
  • Enhanced operational health and security
  • Minimized risk and lower TCO
